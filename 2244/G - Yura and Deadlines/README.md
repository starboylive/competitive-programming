For each assignment, its weight $$$a_i$$$ is known — the number of course points Yura will receive if he completes it.</p><p>Yura wants to choose a subset of assignments to maximize the total number of points. However, he has one problem: some assignments are too time-consuming. If Yura works on two assignments at positions $$$i$$$ and $$$j$$$ ($$$i \neq j$$$), there must be enough other assignments between them; otherwise, he will get distracted and fail to complete them.</p><p>Formally, for any two chosen assignments with indices $$$i$$$ and $$$j$$$, the following condition must hold: $$$|i - j|  \gt  \max(a_i, a_j)$$$.</p><p>Find the maximum total weight Yura can obtain by choosing a subset of assignments satisfying this condition.</p></div><div class="input-specification"><div class="section-title">Input</div><p>The first line contains a single integer
